Biomass Explainer

When should I use partner assistance?

Partner assistance is useful when you have biomass to move, but need help with consolidation, timing, or logistics before it becomes a strong direct marketplace listing.

Use partner assistance when

Your supply is split across sites, your volume is inconsistent, or hauling and staging need coordination before buyers can reliably evaluate it.

List directly when

You control the material, know the quantity and quality, and can publish a clear listing that buyers can respond to without another coordination layer.

What this does not mean

It does not mean there is fake partner inventory sitting in the marketplace. It means the platform can help route fragmented supply toward a more workable path.
