How it works

From browse to delivery — the full buying process.

01

Browse or get matched

Create a free account, answer a few questions about your crops and soil, and get ranked producer matches. Or browse all listings and filter by feedstock, region, and certification.

02

Send an inquiry

Found a listing that fits? Open a deal room directly from the listing card and contact the producer. No intermediary — you communicate directly.

03

Negotiate directly

Agree on volume, price, and delivery terms. The buyer arranges pickup or the producer arranges delivery — agreed between both parties before confirming.

04

Confirm delivery

Once material is received and accepted, both parties confirm delivery through the platform. Quality disputes must be raised within 7 days with lab evidence. No returns after confirmed delivery.

05

Transaction complete

The transaction is marked complete, a verified badge is added to the producer listing, both parties leave a rating, and commission is invoiced to the producer on completed transactions only.
